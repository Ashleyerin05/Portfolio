About Me

I bring structure to ambiguity and make the process feel human along the way.

I'm a junior at Boston University's Questrom School of Business, concentrating in Information Systems & Artificial Intelligence. I love the space where strategy meets execution. Whether that's managing the logistics of distributing 6,000 stationery sets to Rikers Island through ShareLove.Fund or mapping AI tool workflows at an ops startup in Sydney, I'm happiest when I'm building something that actually works.

Outside of work, I am a yoga instructor, I cook a lot, and I just got back from living in Australia, which stretched my perspective in the best way.

I also lead a project that sends art supplies and letters to incarcerated women at Rikers Island. That work keeps me grounded and reminds me why empathy matters in every room I walk into.
